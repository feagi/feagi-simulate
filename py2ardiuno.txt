Python to ardiuno/ros2

More detail information: https://realpython.com/arduino-python/

Steps being done:
1) nano py2ardiuno.py
Copy and paste this in the py2ardiuno.py
#!/usr/bin/env python3
"""
 Blinks an LED on digital pin 13
 in 1 second intervals
"""

from Arduino import Arduino
import time

board = Arduino("/dev/ttyACM0",baudrate=115200, timeout=3.0) # plugged in via USB, serial com at rate 115200
board.pinMode(11, "OUTPUT")

while True:
    board.digitalWrite(11, "LOW")
    time.sleep(1)
    board.digitalWrite(11, "HIGH")
    time.sleep(1)

2) chmod +x py2ardiuno.py
3) python3 py2ardiuno.py
